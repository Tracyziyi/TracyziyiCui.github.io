Analysis of urban planning

This project looks at the evolution of modern urban planning, analyzing urban planning schemes and their impact over time.

Planning Features

While urban planning in the early 20th century paid more attention to functional zoning, modern planning emphasizes more on mixed use and sustainable development. The new planning scheme gives due consideration to the layout of public transportation and green space systems.

Cultural Landmarks Study

This project traces the history of the city's important cultural landmarks, documenting their changing roles in the city's development.

Cultural Value

Studies have shown that these historical landmarks are not only carriers of urban memory, but also an important force in promoting urban renewal and cultural heritage. Many historic buildings have been successfully transformed into modern cultural spaces.
